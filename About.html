Why choose Arc?

What's so good about Arc?/ What does arc have that other social media platform doesn't already have?

That's a good question that we even asked ourselves in the beggining of this project. We asked ourselves "What is something we could create that already hasn't been created with the consumer in mind" and here you have it:
Arc is a social networking platform that brings all your social networks into one. In arc you have access to your Facebook account, All your Twitter accounts, and Instagram accounts! If someone else on Instagram for example is using arc too! You will notice a button for messaging that person directly using Arc! Yes. Arc has the ability to send messages just like telegram, multimedia, all that. Your connection is encrypted on your phone side, but also have the option to turn it off if you lack hardware or connectivity issues. But you will be prone to getting spied on.

Do I need to create an account to be able to use Arc?

You don't need an arc account, you just need a existing social media account thus preventing you from creating another account you YET Have to remember.
